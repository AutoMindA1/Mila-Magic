The 60-Mile Variable

Moreno Valley to Valencia is 60 mi / 1 hr without traffic, 1.5–2 hrs with LA traffic. Plan around it.

🎢

Season Passes

Admission is $0 with season passes. Budget $100–$150/day for park food for 6 people.

The Compromise Structure

Hotel is your base for park days. Family visit is front-loaded so you end strong at the park.

🏨

2 Rooms Minimum
